Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

About This App

AI Tech Feed Poster is a private automation tool used to publish content to Instagram via the Meta API. It is not available to the public.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app only interacts with the Instagram account owned by the app administrator.

Data Usage

No user data is collected or processed. The app solely publishes pre-approved content to a designated Instagram account.

Third Party Services

This app uses the Meta Graph API to publish content to Instagram. Meta's own privacy policy applies to all interactions with their platform.
